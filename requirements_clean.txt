Suggested weights Ɣ industry fit = 30 Ɣ size fit = 20 Ɣ geography fit = 10 Ɣ structure fit = 15 Ɣ buyer type fit = 15 Ɣ activity recency = 5 Ɣ relationship strength = 5 Total = 100 Scoring definitions Industry fit Ɣ exact subindustry match = full score Ɣ same industry, different subindustry = medium - high Ɣ adjacent industry = medium Ɣ unrelated = low/zero Size fit Compare listing revenue/EBITDA/EV to buyer target range.
